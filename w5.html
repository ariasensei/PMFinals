üìã Project Closure Steps

What is Project Closure?

Project closure is the formal process of completing all project activities, delivering final products, and officially ending the project. It's like the final chapter of a book - it brings everything together and provides a satisfying conclusion.

Key Point: Proper closure ensures that nothing is left hanging, all stakeholders are satisfied, and valuable lessons are captured for future projects.
